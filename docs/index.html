The Philosophy of Single-Tasking

In a world of constant distraction, Pomodoro is built on the belief that true productivity comes from doing one thing exceptionally well. No clutter, no noise, just you and your work.

Native by Design

Built with Swift and AppKit to feel like a part of macOS, not just another app running on it.

Single-Task Focus

Our "Task Shelf" ensures you're always focused on exactly one goal at a time.

Privacy First

Your data is yours. Everything is stored locally on your device, ensuring total privacy.

The Pulse

Glanceable, sub-second precision.

Mirror your focus directly in the macOS menu bar. Our zero-drift engine ensures absolute accuracy, while the 60fps progress ring provides a smooth, analogue sweep that feels alive on your screen.

The Shelf

Boutique planning.

A slide-up drawer for managing your goals without distraction. Assign estimated sessions with a tactile stepper and track your momentum with "Pomo Dots" in a high-fidelity interface.

The Insight

High-fidelity reporting.

Visualize your productivity with elegant bar charts and project-level distributions. Gain deep insights into where your time goes with a beautifully crafted analytics dashboard.

The Breath

Analogue relaxation.

During breaks, organic blobs shift in a 4-7-8 breathing rhythm. An experience that makes you calm and relaxed. Try for yourself!

Quick Start Guide

Experience high-fidelity focus in three simple steps.

1

Download

Grab the latest Universal Binary and move it to your Applications folder.

2

Initialize

Because the app is ad-hoc signed, Right-Click the app and select Open for the first launch.

3

Authorize

Open System Settings > Privacy & Security and click Open Anyway under the Security section.

Note: Ad-hoc signing is used for this boutique release. This process bypasses the "unidentified developer" warning safely.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free forever?

Yes. Pomodoro is a labor of love for the community. The core features will always remain free and open source for personal use.

Where is my data stored?

Your privacy is paramount. All focus logs, tasks, and project data are stored locally on your Mac in a native database. Nothing ever leaves your machine.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely. You can export your entire focus history to a CSV file at any time for your own analysis or backup.

Is it open source?

Yes, the code is available on GitHub under a Non-Commercial Share-Alike license. You are welcome to contribute or build it yourself.
